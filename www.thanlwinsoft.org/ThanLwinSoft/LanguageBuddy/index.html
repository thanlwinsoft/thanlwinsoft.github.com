Language Buddy

A Tool for learning a foreign language

Language Buddy is a software self-test tool to help you learn words and phrases in a foreign language. You can group vocabulary into modules and then test yourself by:

  • Listening to the word in the foreign language (if you have a recording)
  • Reading the word in the foreign language and trying to remember the meaning
  • Prompting in your own language so you have to translate it into the foreign language

You mark your self right or wrong when the answer is displayed. Wrong answers will be displayed repeatedly until you get it right. You then have the option to retest all your mistakes.

Key features:

  • Remembers your previous test results and provides optimized revision tests based on the number of times you have got a word right or wrong.
  • Supports multiple languages including complex scripts
  • Provides a Module editor for creating word entries
  • Recording of words and phrases
  • Associating a picture with a word or phrase
  • Searching of modules
  • Tagging of entries to assist searching across multiple modules
  • Language specific sorting
  • Graphing of your test results
  • Export to HTML and PDF
  • Printing of modules
  • OpenSource - LGPL 2
  • Extendable via Eclipse RCP (for software developers)
