ThanLwinSoft.org

OSS - for Myanmar and beyond

The aim of this website is to provide information on Open Source Software for the country of Myanmar (formerly Burma). At the moment the main focus is providing advice on using Unicode with the Myanmar Language.

Why ThanLwinSoft? The ThanLwin River (သံလွင်မြစ်), otherwise known as the Salween, is one of the major rivers that flows through Myanmar/Burma.
